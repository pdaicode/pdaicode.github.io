Derek Peng Dai

Google site, ORCID, Research Gate

I am broadly interested in Data Mining and Machine Learning, specifically in areas of Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Bioinformatics, Precision Medicine and Automatic Speech Recognition.

Currently, I'm working as a Senior Scientist with Huawei Canada Research Center, AI Capability Lab, working on deep learning with special focus on computer vision applications. I'm also an Adjunct Professor with Department of Electrical, Computer & Biomedical Engineering, Ryerson University. We are looking for talented candidates for multiple Postdoc and Intern positions in our Big Data team.

From Jan 2015 to Dec 2016, I'm with the Department of Computer Science, University of Western Ontario, also affiliated with Robarts Research Institute. I'm working on the modeling and detection of Alzheimer's Disease (AD) with Dr. Femida Gwadry-Sridhar and Prof. Michael Bauer as my Principal Investigators. I'm also working closely with Prof. Michael Borrie, and Dr. Robert Bartha.

From Jan 2014 to Dec 2014, I worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow in Toronto Rehabilitation Institute (TRI), joint affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto. TRI is a member of University Health Network (UHN) and is affiliated with the University of Toronto. University Health Network (UHN) is Canada's largest research hospital, and a major teaching hospital of the University of Toronto. My supervisors are Assit/P Frank Rudzicz (Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto) and A/P Alex Mihailidis (Dept of Occupational Science and Occupational Therapy & Institute of Biomaterials and Biomedical Engineering, University of Toronto).

From July 2012 to Dec 2013, I worked as a Research Associate for Assist/P Ho Shen-Shyang in School of Computer Engineering (SCE) of Nanyang Technological University (NTU). I was with the Center for Computational Intelligence (C2i).

Research Topic

General Research Interests: Machine Learning, Statistical Learning, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, and Pattern Recognition.

Research Focus: Automatic Speech Recognition, Natural Language Processing, Behavior Analysis, and Human-Robot Interaction. I mainly use Matlab, Perl, and C++ in my research. Sample codes of my project can be obtained at GitHub.
