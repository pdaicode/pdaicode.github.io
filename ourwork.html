Research Topic

General Research Interests: Machine Learning, Statistical Learning, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, and Pattern Recognition.

Research Focus: Automatic Speech Recognition, Natural Language Processing, Behavior Analysis, and Human-Robot Interaction. I mainly use Matlab, Perl, and C++ in my research. Sample codes of my project can be obtained at GitHut.

1. Machine Learning in the Diagnosis and Prognostication of Alzheimer's Disease

2. Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)

3. Machine Learning in Behavior Analysis (Human Dynamics)

4. Human-Robot Interaction (HRI)
